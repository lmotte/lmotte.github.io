Luc Brogat-Motte

I am a postdoctoral researcher working under the supervision of Riccardo Bonalli (L2S, CentraleSupélec), and Alessandro Rudi (SIERRA team, INRIA Paris). I study the estimation of controlled stochastic differential equations.

Prior to this, I completed my PhD at Télécom Paris, where I studied kernel methods and statistical learning theory under the supervision of Florence d'Alché-Buc (Télécom Paris) and Juho Rousu (Aalto University).
